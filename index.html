What I do

Music Production

I specialize in EDM, while capable with other genres. I can mix and master, and hold a masterclass.

"Every child is an artist, The problem is how to remain an artist once we grow up"

— Pablo Picasso




I am a fresh graduate of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. My specialty in my degree is web development, particularly frontend design and development. In this field, I am able to apply my creative expertise to technology. During my internship, part of my role is being a quality assurance to the web systems, while being an associate with the developers.

I have an experience as an online independent artist whose main content is music. I am my own management, so I also handle the graphic design, digital arts, video editing, and marketing. In this experience, I approached music and visuals as one art piece.

My new objective is to share my expertise and skills in a professional team to help a business. Whether it's bringing fresh ideas to the table, produce work, or collaborating with teams. Not only is this an opportunity to thrive in the real world and commit to personal growth, but also an opportunity to make an impact to the industry.
