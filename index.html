What I do

Music Production

I mainly produce EDM and other specific genres. I can mix and master, and hold a masterclass.

Digital Arts

Photo/Video Editing

"Every child is an artist, The problem is how to remain an artist once we grow up"

— Pablo Picasso




Started as a gaming channel to a music artist channel. I have been producing music for nearly 6 years, I have been gaining more knowledge on music theory along the way. Over these years, I have worked and collaborated with several artists (fellow DTMers, Digital Artists, Motion Graphic Artists and MV Artists, Vocalists/Singers) releasing different levels of projects throughout my discography. My music have been featured in several content, especially in rhythm games. I used this skill for freelancing in an active community full of content creators. I've released my music on different record labels and platforms, leading me to growth and gaining thousands of listeners.

The rest of the skills I obtained from making gaming content are factors that helped give visuals to my music.

2 | daydream

Initial version of the artwork

One of the hardest tracks I've worked on so far, the artwork only took a day to make. Through image manipulation, I was able to craft this artwork by joining different images and retouch needed areas. This is the album cover for my track "daydream".

I started drawing the characters in my small notebook, it only took a few hours. I loaded up my drawing software, and redraw them in the digital canvas. This is the album cover for my track "Friends".
